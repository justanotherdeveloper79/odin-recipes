Vegetable Biryani

Here is an image for you to see what the Vegetable Biryani is going to look like once you have finished making it!

Description

Veg biryani is a fragrant, flavorful, and elaborate Indian rice dish that serves as a popular, hearty vegetarian main course.
It consists of long-grain basmati rice layered or cooked together with a variety of spiced vegetables, often including carrots, peas, potatoes, and beans.
The dish is renowned for its aromatic qualities, imparted by spices like cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, and saffron, along with fresh herbs like mint and cilantro.

  • Prep Time: 35 mins
  • Cook Time: 30 mins
  • Total Time: 1 hr 5 mins
  • Servings: 8

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter), or olive oil
  • 1 red onion, cut into 1/2-inch dice
  • ½ teaspoon cumin seed
  • 1 (1 inch) piece cinnamon stick
  • 7 peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • ½ cup water
  • ½ cup peas
  • ½ cup diced carrot
  • ½ cup diced potato
  • 1 cube chicken bouillon
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground red chile pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon garam masala
  • ¼ teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 cups basmati rice, rinsed and drained

Steps:

  1. Melt ghee in a large Dutch oven over medium heat. Add onion, and cook until softened, about 3 minutes.
    Stir in cumin seed, cinnamon stick, and peppercorns; cook until the spices are fragrant, and the cumin seeds begin to pop, about 3 minutes.
  2. Stir in ginger garlic paste, tomatoes, and 1/2 cup water.
    Bring to a simmer, and cook until the water has evaporated, about 5 minutes.
    Stir in peas, carrot, and potato.
    Season with chicken bouillon, salt, red chile, black pepper, garam masala, and turmeric.
    Stir well, then cover, and cook for 3 minutes.
  3. Pour in 4 cups water and bring to a boil over high heat.
    Once boiling, stir in basmati rice, reduce heat to medium, recover, and simmer for 10 minutes.
    Reduce heat to low and continue to cook until the rice has softened, 10 to 15 minutes more.
